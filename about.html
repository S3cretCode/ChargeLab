About Project ChargeLab

A Grade 11 combined Chemistry + Physics final project.

Project Overview

Project ChargeLab: From Atoms to Fields is an interactive educational website that connects stoichiometry and electrochemistry (Chemistry 30S) with magnetic fields and electromagnetism (Physics 30S).

Learning Goals

  1. Stoichiometry: Explain the mole concept, balance equations, identify limiting reagents, and understand redox electron transfer.
  2. Electromagnetism: Model how electrons form currents that produce magnetic fields using the right-hand rule.
  3. Quantitative Analysis: Solve problems linking chemical quantities to electrical outputs and magnetic field strengths.
  4. Real-World Applications: Connect concepts to batteries, electromagnets, motors, and other devices.

Target Audience

Grade 11 students in Manitoba taking Chemistry 30S and Physics 30S, aligned with Manitoba Education curriculum outcomes.
